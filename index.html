I'm Jo Smith, a passionate wedding photographer hailing from Dundee, where I graduated from university with a degree in photography. With a cheeky smile and a down-to-earth approach, I love capturing the unique love stories of each couple.

My camera is my trusty companion, and I thrive on the excitement of every wedding. When I'm not behind the lens, you can find me exploring the great outdoors or experimenting in the kitchen with my latest baking adventures.

My goal is to tell your unforgettable story through beautiful, heartfelt images that you'll cherish for a lifetime! Let's create magic together!

Where I'm based



I'm based in Dundee, but my love for photography takes me all over Scotland and beyond! Whether it's a breathtaking Highland wedding or a cozy celebration in a quaint village, I'm thrilled to travel nationwide to capture your unique story. Let's make magic together!
